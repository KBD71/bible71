01. Participation in the Benefits of Redemption

Redemption refers to the grace by which Christ paid the price for all our sins on the cross and liberated us from slavery to sin and Satan. However, this redemption remains abstract unless it is personally applied to each individual.

To "participate in redemption" does not mean we share in the act of dying for sin—that is Christ's work alone. Rather, it means we share in the benefits resulting from that work, such as justification, adoption, sanctification, and the assurance of God's love.

02. The Exclusive Agency of the Holy Spirit

The process of applying Christ’s redemptive work to the believer is exclusively the work of the Holy Spirit. We are spiritually dead and unable to comprehend or desire the things of God on our own. Therefore, it is only through the Spirit sent by the ascended Christ that the salvation purchased on the cross becomes effective in our lives.

03. The Meaning of Effectual Application

The use of the term "effectual" is vital. It indicates that the Spirit’s work is not a mere invitation that could be accepted or rejected by human whim. It is a sovereign act of God that certainly achieves its purpose. As Isaiah 55:11 states, God’s Word does not return void but accomplishes His good pleasure. The application of redemption is irresistible and guaranteed because it rests on the power of God, not the fragility of human choice.

Theological & English Notes

1. Procure vs. Purchase (획득하다 / 값 주고 사다)

두 단어 모두 '얻다'는 뜻이지만, 신학적으로는 그리스도께서 **자신의 생명을 대가(속전, Ransom)**로 지불하여 구원을 성취하셨음을 강조하는 법정적, 상업적 의미를 담고 있습니다.

2. Effectual Application (효과 있는 적용)

'Effectual'은 단순히 '효과적'이라는 뜻을 넘어, **하나님의 주권적인 뜻이 반드시 성취되어 실패함이 없음**을 뜻합니다. 구원이 인간의 수용 여부에 달린 것이 아니라 하나님의 강권적인 역사임을 나타냅니다.

3. Partakers (참여자 / 동참하는 자)

성경 원어적(Koinonia) 의미로 '교제'나 '공유'를 뜻합니다. 그리스도께서 이루신 영광과 복을 성도가 함께 누리게 됨을 강조하는 표현입니다.

4. Abstract vs. Concrete (추상적 / 구체적)

본문 해설 중 'abstract'는 적용되지 않은 구원이 이론에 그칠 수 있음을, 'applied'는 그것이 성도 개인의 삶에 실제적인 사건이 됨을 대조적으로 보여줍니다.
