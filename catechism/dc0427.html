Westminster Larger Catechism 57

[English Version]

Q: What benefits hath Christ purchased by his mediation?

A: Christ, by his mediation, hath procured redemption, with all other benefits of the covenant of grace.

01. The True Meaning of Redemption

In a theological sense, redemption is the act of liberating those held in bondage by paying a specific price, known as a ransom. While secular usage might imply "restraint," biblical redemption means the exact opposite: freedom through payment.

Christ offered His own blood as the ransom to set us free from the slavery of sin and death. As He declared, "the Son of Man came... to give his life as a ransom for many" (Matthew 20:28). This legal and spiritual transaction transfers us from the domain of darkness to the kingdom of light.

02. Satisfying Divine Justice

A critical point in Reformed theology is that the ransom was paid to God the Father, not to Satan. Because God is perfectly just, He cannot simply overlook sin. His holiness demands a penalty.

The sacrifice of Christ is the propitiation that satisfies God's righteous wrath. In His infinite love, God provided the very sacrifice His justice required, harmonizing His attributes and allowing Him to justify the ungodly through faith.

03. The Wealth of Covenant Benefits

Beyond the fundamental act of redemption, Christ's mediation secures every spiritual blessing included in the Covenant of Grace.

  • Justification: Legal acquittal and righteousness.
  • Adoption: Becoming true children of God.
  • Sanctification: Being transformed into holiness.
  • Perseverance: Staying secure in faith until the end.

Theological & English Notes

1. Procure / Purchase (획득하다 / 사다)

신학적으로 이 단어들은 단순히 '얻다'는 뜻을 넘어, 그리스도께서 **자신의 생명을 대가(속전, Ransom)**로 지불하여 법적/상업적 권리를 확보하셨음을 강조합니다.

2. Propitiation (화목제물 / 진노를 달래다)

하나님의 거룩한 공의와 죄에 대한 진노를 누그러뜨리는 제사를 뜻합니다. 공의의 요구를 완전히 만족시킴으로써 하나님과 죄인 사이를 화목하게 만듭니다.

3. "Christ hath purchased..." (고전적 표현)

'hath'는 현대 영어의 'has'에 해당하는 고어 표현입니다. 웨스트민스터 표준문서와 킹제임스 성경(KJV) 특유의 장중한 격식을 느낄 수 있습니다.

4. Legal Transaction (법적 거래)

구원을 단순히 '용서'라는 감정적 차원을 넘어, 죗값이 확실히 지불되고 의로움이 전가된 **확실한 법적 절차**로 이해하게 돕는 표현입니다.
