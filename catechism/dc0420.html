Westminster Confession of Faith 8.8

[English Version]

To all those for whom Christ hath purchased redemption, he doth certainly and effectually apply and communicate the same; making intercession for them, and revealing unto them the mysteries of salvation.

[Korean Version]

그리스도께서 구속을 사주신 모든 사람에게, 그는 그 구속을 확실하고 효과적으로 적용하시고 전달하신다. 그는 그들을 위하여 간구하시며, 그들에게 구원의 신비를 나타내신다.

01. The Certainty of Application

The Work of Christ is not a mere possibility but a definite reality. For everyone Christ redeemed on the cross, He ensures that this redemption is effectually applied. This means no one for whom Christ died will ever be lost; His sacrificial death achieves its intended purpose with absolute certainty.

This application involves the Holy Spirit persuading the elect to believe and obey. It is an internal work of grace that transforms the heart, making the objective work of Christ a subjective reality in the life of the believer.

02. Continuous Intercession and Governance

Christ does not stop at purchasing redemption; He continues His work as our Mediator in heaven. He makes intercession for us, standing before the Father as our Advocate. Through this, He reveals the "mysteries of salvation" to our hearts by His Word and Spirit.

He also governs the hearts of His people. By His almighty power and wisdom, He overcomes all spiritual enemies and temptations, guiding us in ways that are most consonant to His wonderful and unsearchable providence.

03. The Triumph of Divine Wisdom

The way Christ applies redemption is perfectly aligned with His unsearchable dispensation. We may not always understand the timing or the methods God uses in our lives, but we can trust that they are the most wise and appropriate for our salvation and His glory.

  • Spiritual Victory: Christ overcomes all enemies—sin, death, and the devil—on behalf of His people.
  • Grace-driven Obedience: Our obedience is not a result of our own strength but of Christ's Spirit "persuading" us.
  • Eternal Security: The union between Christ's purchase and His application ensures that the covenant of grace is unbreakable.

Theological & English Notes

1. Intercession (간구 / 중보 기도)

승천하신 그리스도께서 하늘 보좌에서 자기 백성을 위해 끊임없이 하나님 아버지께 드리는 기도를 뜻합니다. 이는 우리의 구원이 현재 진행형으로 보호받고 있음을 의미합니다.

2. Consonant (조화로운 / 일치하는)

'Consonant with'의 형태로 자주 쓰이며, 하나님의 행동이 그분의 거룩한 성품이나 지혜로운 계획과 완벽하게 일치한다는 뜻으로 사용되었습니다.

3. Unsearchable Dispensation (측량할 수 없는 섭리)

'Unsearchable'은 인간의 지혜로 다 찾아낼 수 없음을, 'Dispensation'은 하나님이 세상을 다스리고 구원을 베푸시는 경륜이나 섭리를 뜻합니다.

4. "Hath" and "Doth" (고어 표현)

신앙고백서 본문의 'hath'는 'has'를, 'doth'는 'does'를 의미합니다. 17세기 영문의 특징으로, 오늘날 성경이나 신앙 문서에서 장중한 느낌을 주기 위해 그대로 보존하기도 합니다.
